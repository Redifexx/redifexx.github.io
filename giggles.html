Giggles Goofy Gameshow

A 3D platformer with a dark twist.

  • Category Game Development
  • Game Engine Unity
  • Project Date Spring 2025
  • Project Site Itch.io

Overview

Giggles Goofy Gameshow is the biggest game I've worked on so far. The inspiration for this was Squid Game and originally, the exploration aspects of Outer Wilds. As the team and I continued to develop it for the University of Florida's Convergence Event, we figured that it was better to make the total gameplay length more focused, so we pivoted into mascot horror. I worked with a team of 4 artists and 2 programmers, although I ended up working on both sides. My teammate Jaden and I worked on the Unity game itself while the artists kept making us great assets to utilize. I even made some myself! I rigged and animated the player model, worked on a few of the environments, and both placed and optimized all the lights in the game. This was my first time utilizing Unity's Adaptive Probe Volumes! Baking lights in Unity 6 is a breeze. Although getting our Git to work properly took a few weeks, we ultimately fixed all our issues and worked right up until the deadline! Incredibly proud of this one :)
